## Why this might fail
Guide/eBook willingness-to-pay is the softest category on the board — free Twitter threads, blog posts, and the community's own docs substitute almost perfectly, and there is no recurring utility to anchor price. The content dates fast as the tooling changes. Worst of all for the lane, its distribution rides the exact same community funnel as the two existing candidates, so it adds product surface without adding a new channel — concentrating rather than spreading the lane's distribution risk.
