# Template — Candidate Intake (with binding kill-rule fields)

Every candidate gets an intake BEFORE it is built. The three kill-rule fields
are binding: they define, in advance, the conditions under which the candidate
is abandoned and ledgered as a negative result. An intake that cannot state
its own kill conditions — or that flinches from naming its weakest axes — has
not earned the build.
