# Bababoefoe — intake (owner-directed candidate)

> A family of round, ultra-fluffy costumed collectible plush critters (Pirate,
> Chef, Police, Santa, Witch editions) whose real physical features — magnetic
> hands, a hidden keepsake pocket, and a per-edition QR tag — feed a cozy, kid-safe
> fiction. Each edition's QR opens its own origin-story page. **Owner-directed
> concept; physical products sit OUTSIDE the lane's proven digital path.**

## What it is
A branded plush IP + a working digital layer. Agents fully built the digital half
in-repo (brand bible, 5 sample stories, a stdlib static story-site, and an
`editions.json` registry that is the source of truth for printed QR tags). The
physical half — crowdfunded plush, print-on-demand accessories, owner-funded
manufacturing — is entirely OWNER-GATED and lives as a phased money-protocol plan
(`MAKE-IT-REAL-PLAN.md`). Honest framing: this lane's proven wins are one-click
digital artifacts; Bababoefoe's revenue is physical and every step needs owner cash
and/or accounts, so it is scored as an exception, not a lane-fit.

## Kill-rule fields (binding intake rule)
- **Validation signal:** within 60 days / owner-click steps of Phase 1 going live —
  ANY of: (a) a crowdfunded-plush campaign reaching ≥25% of its minimum pre-order
  threshold, (b) ≥3 print-on-demand accessory sales, (c) any single physical sale.
  No signal by the effort cap = ledgered negative; do NOT advance to owner-funded Phase 2.
- **First-ten-customers path (⚑ = owner-gated):** (1) crowdfunded-plush campaign page ⚑;
  (2) Etsy collectible listing ⚑; (3) POD keychain/pillow listings ⚑; (4) the free
  QR story-site as an organic discovery surface (Phase 0, agent-done); (5) owner's own
  network for first backers ⚑. Cold-start honesty: channels 1–3 begin with no audience.
- **Max agent-effort budget (this slice):** 150k tokens incl. CI. Over budget without
  a validation signal = ledgered negative.
- **Conservative revenue estimate:** Phase 0 $0 (content/infra). Phase 1 $0–$150 first
  90 days cold. Phase 2 requires ~$4,500+ owner cash at risk with a real chance of
  unsold inventory. Never overstated.
- **Payback-time estimate:** Phase 0/1 cost is owner TIME, not cash (no upfront). Phase 2
  breaks even only after ~half a 500-unit batch sells (~300 units × ~$9 gross) — unproven;
  do not enter without a Phase 1 demand signal.
- **Compliance gate (NOT agent-verifiable):** children's-product safety (ASTM F963/CPSIA
  US, EN71/CE EU) and specifically MAGNET regulation gate the magnetic-hands feature —
  owner homework with a qualified professional + lab testing. See MAKE-IT-REAL-PLAN → SAFETY/COMPLIANCE.

## Why this might fail
No existing audience, so every physical channel starts cold and the crowdfunded route's
"200 fans" precondition is unmet. Speed-to-first-dollar is months, not days. The
magnetic-hands feature — the concept's signature — is a regulated safety liability in
kids' toys and may have to be dropped or costly-tested. Owner-funded manufacturing puts
real cash at risk against unproven demand. This is a passion-brand bet, not a lane-fit
digital artifact; scored honestly at 2.20 for exactly that reason.
