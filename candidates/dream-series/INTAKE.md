## What it is
A Percy-Jackson-style middle-grade/YA fantasy series (**DREAMLINE**, working title) developed from the owner's own concept and Dutch draft fragment: all dreams are real (glimpses of the past, the future, or parallel universes); a hidden magic of dream-control; two secret factions bending or defending history; a thin line between real and fake where reader and characters alike are sometimes unsure whether they are dreaming. Deliverables: a series bible (world + rules + the Lull perception filter), name options for the owner's four naming decisions, a 3-book arc, a Book-1 chapter outline, and drafts of Book 1 chapters 1–3.
