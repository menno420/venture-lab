# Star Pirates — Book 1 (manuscript)

Full, finished Book 1 manuscript of the middle-grade-leaning **Star Pirates** series, developed from [../concepts/07-star-pirates.md](../concepts/07-star-pirates.md). Three complete, separately-localized editions.

| Language | Title | Words | Folder |
|---|---|---|---|
| English (master) | Star Pirates — Book 1: The Wrong Way Down | 11,401 | [en/](en/) |
| Nederlands | Sterrenpiraten — Boek 1: De verkeerde kant op | 12,080 | [nl/](nl/) |
| Deutsch | Sternenpiraten — Buch 1: Der falsche Weg nach unten | 11,806 | [de/](de/) |

- **Age band:** 7–9 chapter book (see [DECISIONS.md](DECISIONS.md)).
- **How to read it:** each language folder has a `README.md` (front matter + pitch) then chapter files `01`–`10`.
- **Localization, not translation:** the Dutch and German editions re-craft the jokes, idioms and Squish's mistranslation gag to land natively; the owner is Dutch, so the NL edition was the top quality priority.
- **Illustration & every publish path (KDP / print / listing) remain owner-gated** — no art was generated here; see the concept's illustration prompt sheet.

Every creative call and its rationale is logged in [DECISIONS.md](DECISIONS.md); the owner can veto any of them.
