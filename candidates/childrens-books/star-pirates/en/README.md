## Pitch

When four bumbling, catastrophically over-polite aliens crash-land on an island where only pirates live, they do the sensible thing: they bluff their way onto a pirate crew to survive. The trouble is they can't hold a cutlass, they shout the war-cry wrong, the big one is terrified of crabs, and their translator-blob cheerfully mistranslates every threat into an offer of jam. But one glowing, half-hidden gadget at a time, this crew of lost misfits quietly becomes the greatest pirates the seven seas ever fumbled into — winning races they don't understand, making friends by accident, and bouncing a villain into the ocean without spilling a drop of blood. Warm, silly, and secretly big-hearted, *The Wrong Way Down* is a laugh-out-loud read-aloud about the strangest crew on the water discovering that home is the one you build out of borrowed planks and stolen jam.
