## Character-name decisions
- Curator — **Juno in all three languages.** The name is native-plausible in EN, NL (a current Dutch girls' name), and DE; keeping it constant gives co-editions one cover name and honors the packet's series hook (*Juno's Museums* / *Juno's Musea* / *Junos Museen*).
- Big brother (unnamed in the packet) — EN **Theo**, NL **Teun**, DE **Tobi**. Short, current, native names; each pairs a plosive with two syllables so the sibling banter scans the same aloud in all three languages.
- Exhibit names localized as museum labels, not calques: Sky Mirror / de Luchtspiegel / der Himmelsspiegel; Upside-Down Tree / de Ondersteboven-Boom / der Kopfüber-Baum; Worm Rescue Wing / de Afdeling Wormenredding (a Dutch museum says "afdeling", not "vleugel", for a department) / die Wurmrettungs-Abteilung (same logic in German); Splash Gallery / de Spetterzaal / die Platsch-Galerie; the Whale / de Walvis / der Wal.

## Language approach
EN is the master and the voice source of truth; NL and DE are native re-tellings, re-authored for rhythm and idiom rather than literal translations.
- **NL:** museum register played straight in child terms — "aanwinsten" (acquisitions) for the arriving rain, "conservator", "topstuk", "collectie"; idiom over calque ("Regels zijn regels", "die deed alsof het niks was", "Vooraan in de rij"); sign texts in Dutch shop-window style (ALLEEN VANDAAG OPEN — ENTREE: ÉÉN BLAD).
- **DE:** "Ausstellungsstücke" for the arriving rain, "Direktorin" (warmer than "Kuratorin" for a child, and natural German for the person who runs a museum), "der größte Schatz der ganzen Sammlung"; natural particles carry Tobi's skepticism ("doch bloß nasse Löcher", "denn bitte noch vor dem Mittagessen").
- **Onomatopoeia localized:** splash EN SPLOOSH / NL PLONS / DE PLATSCH.
- The closing-line pair ("You don't get to keep the exhibits. You get to keep the looking.") is re-built per language around each language's own verb — NL "De stukken mag je niet houden. Het kijken wel." / DE "Die Stücke darf man nicht behalten. Das Schauen schon." — same shape, native compression.
