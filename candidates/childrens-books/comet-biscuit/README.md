# Crew of the Comet Biscuit — manuscripts

Finished, read-aloud picture-book manuscripts developed from the concept in
[`../concepts/04-comet-biscuit.md`](../concepts/04-comet-biscuit.md).

- **Series:** Crew of the Comet Biscuit
- **Logline:** Four wildly mismatched animals fly a wobbly, cookie-tin-shaped spaceship across a friendly cartoon galaxy, taking any odd job they can crumb their way into — and saving the day by teamwork, snacks, and tape.
- **Age band:** 3–6 (picture book; read-aloud early-reader crossover)
- **Format:** each book ~500–630 words, 15 spreads (32-page picture book)
- **Languages:** English (master) · Nederlands · Deutsch
- **Recurring cast:** Captain Nutmeg (hamster, Captain) · Barnaby (sloth, Navigator) · Sprocket (gecko, Engineer) · Bloop (space-jellyfish, stowaway → honorary crew)

## Notes

- Each book is one self-contained delivery-gone-sideways mission; the crew and the ship are the constant, so the series is endlessly re-runnable.
- Bloop is the quiet throughline: in every book the smallest, shyest crew member turns out to be the one who saves the day — by glowing, by kindness, by simply being there.
- **Illustrations:** ready-to-paste image prompts live in the concept doc; this seat cannot generate or publish images. Each spread carries an `[Illustration: …]` art note for the illustrator.
- **Publishing:** every publish/print/sales path is owner-gated. Nothing here is submitted, priced, or published — these are finished manuscripts for the owner to read and review.
- Naming and localization choices are logged in [DECISIONS.md](DECISIONS.md).
