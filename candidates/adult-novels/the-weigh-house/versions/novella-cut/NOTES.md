## What this version is

A complete, self-contained abridgement of the EN master
(`../../en/the-weigh-house.md`, **36,434 words** measured) down to
**18,984 words** (`wc -w the-weigh-house-novella.md`, honest count,
includes title page/content note/chapter headings). Same protagonist,
same POV, same killer, same investigation logic, same ending, all
16 chapters kept with their original titles. Finished prose only — no
placeholders, no summaries, every cut seam rewritten as connective
prose.

Audience/angle: the tight-single-sitting crime-novella reader (KU
page-read economics favour completion rate); the master remains the
long-form edition. Both editions carry the same vetted positioning.

## Scene-level cut list (what went, and why)

- **Ch2 (Ruling):** press liaison, extended debrief personnel, and most
  of the corridor scene — Nadia's introduction compressed to one beat
  (dorsal releases + "he's very keen to keep you out of it" + the "Mm"
  refusal). Why: the café scene (Ch4) already carries the full
  Sanne–Nadia negotiation; the corridor duplicated it.
- **Ch9 (The Mail):** the Sloterdijk sorting-hall scene (clerk Agata)
  and the poste-restante thread cut entirely; the route to the evidence
  now runs directly next-of-kin → Marije → locker key 114 → Centraal.
  Why: the poste-restante item was a dangling loose end even in the
  master (it is never collected on the page); the locker is the thread
  that pays off.
- **Ch6 (The Houses):** the six-house walk compressed to first house +
  pattern + the Slootweg doorstep encounter (kept whole — it seeds
  Ch12); scaffolding/second-house vignettes cut.
- **Ch16 (coda):** weigh-house meditation, team-status epilogue and the
  ring dive all kept but at roughly half length.
- **Throughout:** interiority/atmosphere passages compressed
  (canal-history digressions, repeated cold/water imagery beyond the
  load-bearing motifs); the threes-count motif, "patient, personal"
  cold, towel/van echo, "Good girl" echo, and the weigh-house
  true-weight theme are all retained.
- **No suspects or clue scenes were cut.** The master has no spare
  secondary suspect; the narrowing logic (three → two → one) is
  preserved verbatim in structure.

## Continuity smoothing (master inconsistencies resolved in this cut)

- **Home:** the master gives Sanne both a Prinsengracht flat (Ch2) and a
  houseboat (Ch7/10/11). The cut standardises on the **houseboat on the
  Kloveniersburgwal** (load-bearing: Joke is a mooring neighbour; the
  night-sighting happens on that canal).
- **Lead count:** master Ch1 says 4+4 blocks, master Ch8 says 2+1. The
  cut makes Ch1 non-specific ("a full, deliberate load") and lets Ch8
  carry the load analysis.
- **Knot naming:** master drifts between "half-hitch" (Ch8) and
  "half-twist" (Ch10); the cut uses **half-hitch** consistently.
- **Evidence timing:** the master has the ledger in a dinghy ballast
  locker on the night of the climax yet in a safe-deposit box hours
  later. The cut moves the securing explicitly to Ch11 (safe-deposit box
  under Nadia's name + FIOD locker) before the Slootweg meeting.
- **Fair-play plant added:** the comms-rig backup SD card (revealed only
  post-hoc in the master's Ch15) is now planted in Ch13's gear-check
  alongside the pony bottle and second cutter.
