# Novella cut — NOTES

## What this is

A complete editorial condensation of the full novel (canonical `en/`, 29,403
words across 12 chapters) to novella length. The full 12-chapter structure,
POV, arc, and ending are preserved; the cut is achieved by scene-level
deletions plus a line-level tightening pass, with new connective tissue
written where cuts created seams (e.g. Chapter 7's protocol insight now rides
on the remembered Chapter 4 recital instead of a second on-page Ropati
visit). No summary placeholders — every line is finished prose.

## What was cut and why (scene level)

- **Ch 1:** Lani's four unanswered phone messages (her introduction now
  happens on-page in Ch 3, where it lands harder); archive-boxing texture
  trimmed to the calendar-with-fish beat.
- **Ch 2:** the extended RFI-history digression (COVID-era quiet, mast
  timelines) collapsed into the "drunk shouting in the street" contrast; the
  Marcus call trimmed of relationship banter that Ch 8 restates.
- **Ch 3:** the afternoon receiver-repair sequence cut (pure texture); the
  fitting montage compressed to line/parabola/sinusoid.
- **Ch 4:** ice-core and eclipse-record methodology compressed to their
  findings; the star-paths passage tightened. The Ropati recital scene — the
  book's engine — kept nearly whole.
- **Ch 5:** the four-clocks recap opening cut (redundant with Ch 4); the
  Marcus midnight call kept but shortened to the hold-it-or-go-public
  decision.
- **Ch 6:** the deep-time tempo meditation compressed; the clinic scene kept
  to the ECG-as-timing image and the word "months".
- **Ch 7:** Ropati's second on-page visit (two days before) cut entirely —
  the protocol insight now triggers off the remembered Ch 4 recital; the
  "no institution lasts" litany shortened.
- **Ch 8:** the Halvorsen call reduced to its function (the flag + the
  warning); the intimacy scene kept but further restrained; the dawn-insight
  dialogue kept nearly whole.
- **Ch 9:** Tuli's gafa-as-error-correcting-code speech shortened (the reader
  has already seen the recital); the ma'au recovered-memory beat kept.
- **Ch 10:** the fono compressed (Malaefou's whale/aiga exchange kept, the
  two-dissenting-matai negotiation shortened); Lani's choice scene kept
  nearly whole — it is the hinge of the ending.
- **Ch 11:** ceremony build-up compressed; the send, the new verse, and the
  "ending is how continuing is done" passage kept.
- **Ch 12:** the off-island-visitors paragraph cut; Lani's late-life summary
  compressed; the answering kept whole.

Line-level: throughout, doubled imagery and second beats of already-landed
ideas were removed; no plot event, no named character, and no chapter was
dropped.
