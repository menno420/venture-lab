**Title:** The Marginalia Society · **Category:** YA / dark academia
mystery · **Date vetted:** 2026-07-13 (concept-stage)

**One-line pitch:** At a scholarship boarding school, secrets are passed the
only way the library's surveillance can't read them — as penciled notes in
the margins of books nobody checks out. When a new scholarship student
cracks the annotation code, she inherits a decades-old conversation, and the
question of what happened to the last student who wrote in these margins.
YA dark academia mystery: libraries, class lines, and reading as trespass.

**Catalog fit (why this tiles):** the YA shelf currently reserves folk dark
fantasy (Hollowtide), post-collapse radio drama (Last Good Frequency), and
coastal psychological suspense (Undertow) — school/library/secret-society
mystery is an adjacent, unclaimed register. Distinct from The Painted
Stones' *children's* mystery node (Teen & YA shelf vs Children's shelf) and
from Hollowtide's supernatural dark (this is non-supernatural).
