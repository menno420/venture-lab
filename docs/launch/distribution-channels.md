# Distribution channels shortlist

> **Status:** `reference`

Every external channel below needs an owner account and owner time, so each is flagged OWNER-ACTION — no agent pursues them. The only zero-account channel is in-repo linking. All expected yields are conservative and low; do not assume traffic.

| Channel | Effort | Cost | Owner account needed? | Conservative expected yield | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| In-repo README buy badge | Low | $0 | No | Near-zero traffic, but free | Agent-doable once the live URL exists |
| Gumroad / Lemon Squeezy Discover | Low | ~10% fee | YES — OWNER-ACTION | Low without reviews | Marketplace surfacing depends on sales/reviews you don't have yet |
| Show HN | Low | $0 | YES — account | Spiky: 0 to a few hundred visits | One shot; honest framing only |
| Reddit (r/SaaS, r/Entrepreneur, r/ClaudeAI) | Low | $0 | YES + self-promo rules | Low, removal risk | Subreddit self-promo rules bite; expect removals |
| Indie Hackers | Low | $0 | YES | Low | Community post; low reach without a following |
| Dev.to / Hashnode article from the demo transcript | Medium | $0 | YES | Long-tail SEO, low | Slow-burn; write once from the transcript |
| Product Hunt | Medium-high | $0 | YES | Spiky | High effort for one spike; needs assets and timing |
| Owner's existing X / LinkedIn | Low | $0 | YES — owner posts | Depends on following | Cheapest reach if the owner already has an audience |

Meaningful distribution requires owner accounts and owner time; conservative yield is low across every channel. Do not assume traffic. Flag any channel needing owner money or new accounts as OWNER-ACTION — do not pursue.
