## 💡 Session idea

💡 The v0.2 win isn't "we added a database" — it's that persistence arrived as a
*seam*, not a rewrite. One `MembershipStore` interface, two implementations, and
the hosted path (`SupabaseStore`) ships as a documented skeleton whose method
bodies carry the exact PostgREST request shape inline. Going live is a config
flip (`STORE_BACKEND=supabase` + keys) plus filling those bodies — `app.py`
never changes. That means an agent can build the restart-safe local product
today with zero accounts, and the owner's later Supabase step is a bounded,
pre-scoped fill-in-the-blanks task rather than a refactor.

## ⟲ Previous-session review

⟲ previous-session review: PR #5 shipped candidate #1 v0.1 — a mock-demonstrable
