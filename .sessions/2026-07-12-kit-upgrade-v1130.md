## 💡 Session idea

`bootstrap.py upgrade` could end by printing a one-line "post-upgrade strict
delta" — running `check --strict` itself and diffing findings before/after the
upgrade — so a distribution session sees immediately which findings are
upgrade-caused (e.g. a replanted doc arriving orphaned) versus pre-existing,
instead of discovering them one strict-run later. Cheap to add in the kit,
saves a diagnosis step in every consumer repo per release.
