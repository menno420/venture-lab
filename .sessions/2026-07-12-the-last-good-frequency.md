## 💡 Session idea

Radio gives the book a distinctive, packageable form (italic on-air interstitials open every chapter) and a natural engine for the theme: finding your literal voice. Twelve chapters, ~2,200 words each, written to a locked story bible so voice and canon hold across the manuscript.
